For starters, this gym is family oriented. We expect every member to feel as though this is their second home. Everything is treated with respect here, from the staff, to the members, to the equipment. Coupled with this, every single person within the community we have cultivated wants to see one another succeed. The overall positivity, respect and hospitality that resides within this gym is unmatched and is the leading cause of members coming back for generations.

Another factor that many people take into account is the array of equipment a gym has and the quality of the equipment. Here at Atlantic Fitness we take pride in the range of options anyone has when they step into our facility. There are four distinct sections within the gym one of them being our free weights and machines section. Our machines and free weights allow members to take their workouts to any level if intensity. The cardio section of our gym features cardio machines such as treadmills and stair masters along with a track that wraps around the whole second floor. Another popular feature of our gym is the basketball court which is enclosed on our first floor. Lastly, we have a boxing area with punching bags and a speed bag. All of these sections can be seen in the gallery tab.
